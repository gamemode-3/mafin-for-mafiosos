# Definition
Let $f: X \to Y$ and $g: Y \to Z$ be [[Mappings over Sets|mappings]].
The ___composition___ of $X$ and $Y$ is the mapping $g \circ f : X \to Z$ where $(g \circ f)(x) = g(f(x))$
