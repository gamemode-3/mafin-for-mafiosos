# Definition
A [[Set|set]] $Y$ is a superset of a set $X$ if $x \in X$ implies $x \in Y$.
# Notation
- $X \supset Y$ denotes that $X$ is a superset of $Y$
- $X \subset Y$ denotes that $Y$ is a superset of $X$
