# Definition
A [[Set|set]] $X$ is a subset of a set $Y$ if $x \in X$ implies $x \in Y$
# Notation
- $X \subset Y$ denotes that $X$ is a subset of $Y$
- $X \supset Y$ denotes that $Y$ is a subset of $X$
